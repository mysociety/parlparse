Select Committee Membership

Select Committees are appointed for the duration of a parliament. Following the election on 5 May 2005, the House has appointed MPs to serve on each committee; for a few select committees this has not yet occurred.  Details of the new committee memberships will be published below as soon as they have been appointed.

Until new members are appointed the details of membership from before the general election can be found on the individual committee pages.
